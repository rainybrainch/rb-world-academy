CONTENT EXAMPLE: LESSON 1 VOCABULARY
================================================================================

Term: 你好 (nǐ hǎo)
Definition: Hello / Hi
Traditional: 你好
Simplified: 你好
Pinyin: nǐ hǎo
Tone Marks: nǐ = 3rd tone (falling-rising), hǎo = 3rd tone (falling-rising)
Synonyms: 各位好 (gè wèi hǎo), 大家好 (dà jiā hǎo)
Antonyms: (none - this is a greeting, not an antonym concept)
Example: 你好！很高兴认识你。
Translation: Hello! Nice to meet you.
Notes: Most common general greeting, can be used at any time
Cultural Context: Standard Mandarin greeting; shows respect and friendliness
