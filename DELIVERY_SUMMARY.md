## 🎨 CONTENT SAMPLES

### Sample A1 Vocabulary Entry

```
Term: 好 (hǎo) - Good/Well

Simplified:   好
Traditional:  好
Classical:    佳 / 善
Pinyin:       hǎo (third tone)
English:      Good, well, fine

Category:     Common adjective
Difficulty:   A1 (Beginner)

Example Sentences:
- Simplified:  这是好主意。(This is a good idea.)
- Traditional: 這是好主意。(This is a good idea.)
- Classical:   此為佳策也。(This is a good strategy.)

Usage Context:
- Can precede nouns: 好人 (good person), 好天气 (good weather)
- Common in greetings: 早上好 (good morning)
- Standalone in positive responses: 好！(Okay!)

Cultural Note:
- 好 is one of the first characters learned
- Appears in many common compounds
- Character shows woman (女) with child (子), symbolizing harmony
```

### Sample A2 Dialogue

```
Context: Ordering food at a restaurant

Person A:
├─ Simplified:  你好！我要一份麻辣鸡。
├─ Traditional: 你好！我要一份麻辣雞。
├─ Classical:   安好！吾欲一碟辛香雞肉。
├─ Pinyin:      Nǐ hǎo! Wǒ yào yī fèn málà jī.
└─ English:     Hello! I'd like one spicy chicken.

Person B:
├─ Simplified:  好的。饭还是面？
├─ Traditional: 好的。飯還是麵？
├─ Classical:   諾。飯乎麵乎？
├─ Pinyin:      Hǎo de. Fàn háishi miàn?
└─ English:     Okay. Rice or noodles?

Person A:
├─ Simplified:  我要米饭，谢谢。
├─ Traditional: 我要米飯，謝謝。
├─ Classical:   吾欲米飯，謝汝。
├─ Pinyin:      Wǒ yào mǐ fàn, xiè xie.
└─ English:     I want rice, thank you.
```

### Sample B1 Idiom Analysis

```
Idiom: 卧虎藏龙 (wò hǔ cáng lóng)

Literal Translation:
- 卧 = lying down, dormant
- 虎 = tiger
- 藏 = hidden, concealed
- 龙 = dragon
- Meaning: "Lying tiger, hidden dragon"

Modern Interpretation:
Hidden talents and potential that could emerge powerfully if unleashed

Origin:
- Based on concepts from 易经 (I Ching)
- Reflects Taoist philosophy of latent power
- Connected to 阴阳 (yin-yang) balance

Usage Context:
- Describing organizations with untapped potential
- Referring to individuals with hidden abilities
- Used in business and management discussions

Examples:
- "这个小公司卧虎藏龙，迟早会成为行业领导者。"
  (This small company has hidden dragons; it will eventually become a leader in the industry.)

Related Idioms:
- 龙跃凤飞 (dragon leaping, phoenix flying) - reaching peak potential
- 卧虎待时 (lying tiger awaiting its time) - patience before action
```
