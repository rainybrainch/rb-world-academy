================================================================================
                         EDUCATIONAL FOUNDATION
================================================================================

This expansion is based on proven language acquisition theory:

✓ CONSTRUCTIVISM
  Students learn by building meaning in context, not passive reception

✓ COMPREHENSIBLE INPUT/OUTPUT
  Examples + context provide rich input; 3-tier practice enables output at level

✓ SPACED REPETITION
  Vocabulary appears in glossary, examples, and practice problems

✓ SCAFFOLDING
  Three-tier practice (basic → intermediate → advanced) with supports

✓ AUTHENTIC LANGUAGE
  Native speaker sections show real patterns, not textbook perfection

✓ COMMUNICATIVE COMPETENCE
  Beyond grammar: cultural awareness, register shifting, sociolinguistics
