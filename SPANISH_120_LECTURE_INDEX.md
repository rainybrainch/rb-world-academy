## 🎓 Learning Progression Model

### A1 → A2 (Beginner → Lower Intermediate)
- **Goal**: Independent user in familiar domains
- **Grammar Addition**: Preterite, imperfect, present perfect
- **Vocabulary Growth**: 500 → 1000-1500 words
- **Cultural Shift**: Survival Spanish → Social conventions

### A2 → B1 (Lower → Upper Intermediate)
- **Goal**: Conversational fluency, basic written expression
- **Grammar Addition**: Subjunctive mood, conditional
- **Vocabulary Growth**: 1000-1500 → 2000-2500 words
- **Cultural Shift**: Etiquette → Literary awareness

### B1 → B2 (Upper Intermediate → Advanced)
- **Goal**: Clear expression, argumentation capability
- **Grammar Addition**: Advanced subjunctive, passive structures
- **Vocabulary Growth**: 2000-2500 → 3500+ words
- **Cultural Shift**: Appreciation → Analysis
